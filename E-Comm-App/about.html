About Us

The Information Technology Institute (ITI) is a leading Egyptian organization focused on human capacity development in the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) sector. Established in 1993 and affiliated with the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) since 2005, ITI offers a wide range of training programs and initiatives aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands. These programs cater to various skill levels, from junior to professional, and cover a broad spectrum of ICT specializations
